## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any violations.

Maintainers who are the subject of a report will not participate in the enforcement decision for that report.

## Enforcement Guidelines

Community leaders will follow these guidelines when determining consequences:

### 1. Correction
**Impact:** Minor unprofessional or inappropriate behavior.

**Consequence:** A private or public written warning explaining why the behavior was inappropriate. A public apology may be requested.

### 2. Warning
**Impact:** Repeated or escalated violations.

**Consequence:** A formal warning with clear expectations for future behavior. Continued violations may result in temporary restrictions.

### 3. Temporary Ban
**Impact:** Serious or sustained violations.

**Consequence:** Temporary removal from participation in project spaces for a specified period.

### 4. Permanent Ban
**Impact:** Ongoing harassment, abuse, or severe misconduct.

**Consequence:** Permanent removal from all project-related spaces.
